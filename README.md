Homo Moaning Emulator 🌈
"Where imagery ends, the symphony of desire begins."
There are no visuals here, only the raw purity of sound.
This emulator is an experiment in auditory layering, designed to trace the silhouette of two souls intertwined in the depths of night. It is a digital echo of sweat, racing heartbeats, and the irrepressible cadence of heavy breaths.

⚠️ Usage Note
Due to the high-fidelity and large size of the audio assets, we strongly recommend downloading the repository to your local machine and opening index.html in your browser for the smoothest, uninterrupted experience.
